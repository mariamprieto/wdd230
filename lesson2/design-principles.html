PARC: Proximity

NETFLIX
www.netflix.com

Netflix presents a clear proximity design when we see the connection of the items and content of the page, establishing a relative strength relationship between them, where it is observed that there is organization, relevance between each items and excellent hierarchy.

PARC: Contrast

SPOTIFY
www.netflix.com

Spotify presents a good example of contrast on its website, there is harmony between colors, typography, font sizes and images. as well as a good alignment between the content and the icons.

PARC: Alignment

WALMART
www.walmart.com

The Walmart website presents a vertical alignment of the items, in what is considered the same vertical space between the items and the same direction of the drawings. Also can be seen in the optical alignment between the circles and each sales item, where there is no single position but in harmony with the context.
